About

Personal Summary

As a UX Designer (background in HCI), I am very enthusiastic about UX design and want to create something great that can make a different to people's life.

I have expertise in applying User-Centered Design process and core usability and accessibility principles to a broad range of complex and diverse business scenarios. I have in-depth User-Centred Design experience in mobile, tablet and web application design.

I am highly creative with an eye for innovation and detail, yet still maintain a consistent scientific outlook and methodical approach.

Moreover, I have strong communication skills, and collaborate well within a team in an agile development environment, particularly with software developers due to my previous IT background. I know how to present compelling UCD project cases to a diverse range of audiences.

Qualifications:

  • MSc in Human-Centred Interactive Technologies
  • BSc (Hons) in Information Technology (IT)
  • BSc E-commerce

Outside of the office
